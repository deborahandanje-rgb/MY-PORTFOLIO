Sauti Salama

Silence protects offenders—communities need a digital platform that turns voices into action and accountability.

The Challenge

Many survivors of sexual and gender-based violence face barriers in accessing justice, legal information, and community support. Silence, stigma, and limited access to resources often prevent survivors from speaking out.

Who It Serves

  • Survivors of sexual violence
  • Young people and communities
  • Advocates and support organisations
  • Human rights defenders
  • Community leaders and educators

The Solution

Sauti Salama is a community-centred digital platform designed to provide legal education, survivor support, and awareness resources that promote accountability, safety, and informed action.

Core Pillars of Sauti Salama

Legal Rights

Empowering individuals with knowledge of their rights and available legal pathways.

Community Support

Building supportive communities that listen, protect, and advocate for survivors.

Education

Providing accessible educational resources that promote awareness and prevention.

Safety

Promoting safe spaces where individuals can seek information and support.

Accountability

Encouraging collective responsibility and action to address violence and injustice.

Project Status

Sauti Salama is currently in the design and development phase as part of my web development and social innovation journey. The platform continues to evolve with the goal of creating safer and more informed communities.

30Nine Visuals

Celebrating African creativity through contemporary art, culture, and visual storytelling.

"Every culture has a story worth preserving. Every artist has a voice worth sharing."

The Inspiration

Inspired by Bungoma County, Kenya (County No. 39), 30Nine Visuals is a creative venture that seeks to celebrate African stories, artistic expression, and contemporary design through digital and visual media.

What is 30Nine Visuals?

30Nine Visuals is an online creative space dedicated to showcasing contemporary African art, digital illustrations, and culturally inspired designs that connect creativity with storytelling.
